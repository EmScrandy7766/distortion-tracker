# Distortion Tracker

A lightweight tracker for Destiny 2's **Distortions** activity. Since the final update (Monument of Triumph, June 2026), the Distortion rotates through seven destinations hourly on the hour in a fixed order:

**Cosmodrome → EDZ → Dreaming City → Throne World → Moon → Europa → Nessus**

This page tracks the rotation, notifies you before shifts, and keeps loot reference tables for every destination.

## Quick start

1. **Calibrate** — check your Director in-game for the red Distortion circle, then tap that destination in the Calibrate panel. One tap; the whole schedule is computed from it. You only need to redo this if it ever drifts.
2. **Enable notifications** — click *Enable notifications* and allow the browser prompt. Pick a warning lead time (1–15 min).
3. **Set a farm filter (optional)** — toggle the destinations you care about and you'll only get alerts when one of those is about to go live. Leave all off to be alerted for every shift.
4. **Track your armor** — expand a destination under *Loot by Destination* and tap armor pieces as they drop. Progress is saved in your browser.

## What's included

- Live countdown ring with the current and next destination
- Full 7-hour schedule in your local time
- Per-destination loot tables: weapon pools (with element icons) and armor sets with their 2-piece / 4-piece set bonuses
- Subclass keywords color-coded by element

## Good to know

- **Notifications only fire while the tab is open.** A background/pinned tab is fine; a closed tab is not.
- **Settings are per-browser.** Calibration, farm filters, and armor progress are stored locally on your device — each person calibrates once on their own.
- No accounts, no API keys, no tracking. It's a single HTML file.
